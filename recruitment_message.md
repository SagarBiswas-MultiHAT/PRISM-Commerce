## Option 1: English (Startup & Tech Focused)

**Subject: Let's build the next big thing in BD E-commerce — PRISM Commerce V4 🚀**

Hey everyone! 👋

Over the last few months, I’ve been working on something massive. I've evolved our PRISM Commerce project from a simple academic assignment into **V4.0: a full-fledged, production-ready startup plan**. 

The vision is to build Bangladesh's first **Bangla-native, AI-agentic multi-vendor marketplace** that actually solves local problems for sellers and buyers—aiming to be a hyper-local, zero-cost alternative to Daraz and Shopify. 

I’ve mapped out the entire architecture, from AI models to financial escrow. We are going to build this over the next 15 months, mostly self-funded (just splitting server costs), and I am looking for **3 Co-Founders** to build this with me as an equal team. It will be pure sweat equity—what we build, we own.

Here are the specific founding roles I’m looking to fill (I'll be taking the Full-Stack Architect & AI Lead role):

📱 **Member B — Frontend Lead & Mobile Developer (22% Equity)**
You will own the face of PRISM! You'll be working with Next.js 15, React Native (Expo), and Tailwind. Your domain includes the bilingual web app (PWA), short-form video reel feeds, live commerce UI, offline POS UI, and the complete cross-platform mobile app.

🏦 **Member C — Backend Dev for Payments, Compliance & Logistics (22% Equity)**
You will own the money and trust layers! You'll be integrating bKash, Nagad, SSLCommerz, the upcoming Bangla QR, and our Escrow system. You'll also handle the intelligent 3PL routing engine (Pathao, RedX) and the complete WhatsApp Cloud API integration.

📈 **Member D — QA Engineer & Growth/Business Dev (16% Equity)**
You are the safeguard and the growth engine! You'll own our automated testing suite (Jest, Playwright) to ensure zero critical bugs. Beyond code, you will lead vendor acquisition in Dhaka markets and content creation for our 'PRISM Academy' to educate our sellers.

I have the entire 15-month roadmap, revenue gates, and technical architecture clearly documented. Let me know which seat excites you the most, and let’s sit down this week to go over the complete V4.0 blueprint! 

Who's in? 🔥
