# Walkthrough: PRISM Commerce V3.0 → V4.0 Upgrade

> **Date:** April 2026  
> **Scope:** Production-grade competitive platform transformation

## What Changed

PRISM V3.0 was a strong startup plan. V4.0 makes it **investor-ready, market-ready, and regulation-ready** after deep competitive analysis against Daraz, Shopify, Netstock, Sheba.xyz, and Chaldal.

## Research Conducted

1. **Daraz Bangladesh (2025-2026)** — DarazMall (3x cashback), Choice channel, DEX logistics + IoT DigiBox, DarazLive, Seller University, marketing DMS
2. **Shopify (2026)** — Sidekick (agentic AI), Magic (brand voice), Hydrogen+Oxygen (headless), MCP protocol, agentic storefronts
3. **Netstock** — Opportunity Engine™ (90-second refresh), Pivot Forecasting®, automated SKU classification
4. **Sheba.xyz/sManager** — POS for SMEs, loan access, credit management, Bangla-native
5. **Bangladesh Market (2026)** — Bangla QR mandate (June 2026), escrow requirement, 75% mobile internet, 60% MFS adoption

## New in V4.0

### New Strategic Modules
- **Agentic Sahayak V2** — Function calling, action execution, voice commands
- **Opportunity Engine** — Proactive pricing intelligence every 15 minutes
- **Bundle Recommendation Engine** — Co-purchase pattern analysis
- **Short-Form Video Commerce** — TikTok-style shoppable reels
- **POS Mode** — Barcode scan, unified online+offline inventory
- **PRISM Academy** — Bangla video tutorials, certification, onboarding wizard
- **Escrow System** — Regulatory-compliant payment holding
- **PRISM Verified** — 3-tier vendor verification with badges
- **PRISM Protect** — Buyer protection guarantee + dispute resolution
- **COD Intelligence** — Fraud scoring, history tracking, digital incentive
- **Bangla QR** — National standardized QR payments
- **Referral Growth Engine** — Vendor/buyer/affiliate referral programs

### Architecture Upgrades
- CQRS (Command Query Responsibility Segregation)
- Event Sourcing for orders, payments, inventory
- Saga pattern for distributed transactions
- Distributed tracing via OpenTelemetry
- Read replicas for analytics
- API versioning
